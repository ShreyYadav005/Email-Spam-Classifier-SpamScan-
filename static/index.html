How it
works

Four steps, milliseconds apart. From raw text to a confident verdict.

01
🔤
TF-IDF Vectorization

Converts raw email text into numerical feature vectors, giving higher weight to rare but meaningful words.

02
🧠
Naive Bayes Classifier

Applies Bayes' theorem with word independence assumptions to compute spam and ham probabilities.

03
🔗
Bigram Features

Captures word pairs like "free money" or "click here" for richer contextual understanding.

04
📊
Confidence Score

The model outputs a calibrated probability — not just a label — so you see exactly how certain it is.
